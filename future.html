What I've been doing to mine.
Innovation in society is most important to me, we would not be where we are today without it, and I take it seriously. Undoubtedly I want to be the next Elon Musk, the next Aaron Levie; I want to revolutionize the world through science and technology. This thought occurred to me initially in 2016, so I made a choice to write my first research paper on that was focused on renewable energy.
Although the new method I proposed ended up being roughly twelve times more expensive and around twenty times less efficient than common solar panels, it was still a success in my book, a success for taking my first step into the realm of innovation.
